
EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK - ENGINEERING DEPARTMENT

Section 1: Compensation Policy
All employees in the engineering department receive a base salary review every 6 months.
Senior engineers are eligible for performance bonuses up to 20 percent of annual salary.
Interns receive a fixed stipend and are not eligible for bonuses.

Section 2: Manager Guidelines
Managers are responsible for conducting quarterly performance reviews.
Budget approvals above 10000 dollars require manager sign-off.
Managers have access to team salary information for their direct reports.

Section 3: General Benefits
All staff including interns receive health insurance coverage.
Remote work is allowed up to 3 days per week for all employees.
Annual learning budget of 1000 dollars applies to employees and above.
